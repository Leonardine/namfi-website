The Namibian Maritime and Fisheries Institute

Welcome

The oldest profession in the world is to be a seafarer. To become a professional seafarer you can join the Namibian Maritime and Fisheries Institute for all your maritime training.

If you are keen on following a career on sea or in related marine industries, then you have reached the right web site. The Namibian Maritime Fisheries Institute (NAMFI) is Namibia’s premier training institution for all seagoing personnel

The vision of NAMFI is: “to be the maritime and fisheries training centre of choice in the SADC region”.

The mission statement is: “to meet the needs of all stakeholders by providing high quality maritime and fisheries training in accordance with international standards”.
